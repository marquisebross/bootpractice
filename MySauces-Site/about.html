A Little Somethin' About Us

Rosses Sauces is a gourmet sauce company originated in California, specializing in signature, hand crafted sauces. Every sauce is prepared in house by Cultural Chef Marquise Ross (Le Cordon Bleu, San Fransisco California). Each recipe has been taste tested and altered to perfection over many years by many mouths. All sauces are made with fresh and natural ingredients and inspired by different cultures all over the world!
